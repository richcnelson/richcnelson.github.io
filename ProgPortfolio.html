Mineral Finder Bot

Python 'Bot' program to help people on social media site Reddit with mineral and rock identification. Automated bot searches through comments of reddit comments to find mentions of various rocks, minerals, and crystals. The bot then replies to the parent comment with an informational comment, including details such as Moh's hardness, color, streak, and lustre, as well as including relevant photographs.
